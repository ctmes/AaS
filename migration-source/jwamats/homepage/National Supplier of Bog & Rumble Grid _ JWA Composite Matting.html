Custom Matting Solutions Fit For Every Situation

Soft Ground Access

Interlocked roadways into tidal flats, coastal wetlands, riverbanks, swamps, black soil and muddy farmlands.

Crane Platforms

Temporary platforms suitable for cranes of all sizes - from mobile crane trucks to heavy lift crawler cranes.

Piling Pads

Improve ground bearing capacity for piling works such as sheet piling, bored piling and driven piling.

Oilfield Rig Mats

Composite matting for well sites, campsites, rig access and exploration programs

Trackout Control

Rapid install trackout control matting to keep mud and debris on site

Beach Access

Interlocking mats for access in soft sand beaches. From wheelchair access mats to boat ramps to heavy equipment RO-ROs.

Construction Site Access

Weatherproof critical transport routes to site. Protection of existing roads, footpaths, pavements and heritage tree roots.

Geotechnical Drilling

Access for track mounted or truck mounted rigs to support geotechnical drilling, environmental studies and explorations.

Sensitive Site Access

Temporary roadways with minimal topsoil disturbance in class A nature reserves, protected wetlands, cultural heritage sites, acid-sulfate soils, buried asbestos, and other sensitive grounds.

Pipeline Works

Right-of-way for pipeline and trenching works.

Pedestrian Walkways

Temporary shared user paths for diversion of pedestrian, wheelchair and cyclist traffic.

Event Matting

Hardscape protection, temporary carparks, bump-in/out access, pedestrian walkways.

Case studies from all industries

  • Powerline Stringing - Dulacca Wind Farm

    Dulacca, QLD
    Assisting powerline stringing operations with temporary roadway matting for site access in sensitive agricultural land.

  • Geotechnical Studies on Kooragang Island

    Newcastle, NSW
    How composite swamp mats provided temporary access into mangrove wetlands with known acid sulfate soils.

  • Dundonnell Wind Farm

    Mortlake, VIC
    Dura-Base provided access through sensitive and soft grounds along transmission line corridor for substation construction and powerline stringing works.

    Central Queensland, QLD
    The 3,200T Marion 8050 dragline was scheduled to travel for four months through the rugged outback terrain in regional Queensland, Australia.

  • West Gate Tunnel Project

    West Melbourne, VIC
    800m of light-duty composite matting was installed to be used as a temporary shared-user pathway.

    FODS Trackout Control Mats have landed in Australia and what better place to trial than on two of Melbourne's major infrastructure projects.
